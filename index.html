PROJECT ENVISION


Project Envision, a venture by Enactus in association with CSRD-RS VIT University, centred at Vellore, has a motive of training and employing the visually challenged in a fully functional call centre. The aim of this project is to ensure that barriers are broken down in the community by proving that there is no significant gap in the employability skills of visually handicapped people and everyone else in many fields. Currently, there are multiple projects by various NGOs that focus on helping physically handicapped people but there is a major scarcity of initiatives for visually handicapped.

Through this project, we wish to inspire the visually challenged to use their differently advanced skills to become independent. Employment will not only make them financially independent, but will also help them gain the equal status in the society. Project Envision focuses on achieving a number of UN goals such as reducing poverty, generating employment and economic growth.

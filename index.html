Project Envision, a venture by Enactus in association with CSRD-RS VIT University, centred at Vellore, has a motive of training and employing the visually challenged in a fully functional call centre. The aim of this project is to ensure that barriers are broken down in the community by proving that there is no significant gap in the employability skills of visually handicapped people and everyone else in many fields. Currently, there are multiple projects by various NGOs that focus on helping physically handicapped people but there is a major scarcity of initiatives for visually handicapped.

Through this project, we wish to inspire the visually challenged to use their differently advanced skills to become independent. Employment will not only make them financially independent, but will also help them gain the equal status in the society. Project Envision focuses on achieving a number of UN goals such as reducing poverty, generating employment and economic growth.

What makes it an Enactus project?

The key to Enactus is empowerment and sustainability. Projects need to make sure they pass on skills or knowledge that can be used again without assistance, this is to ensure the solution is sustainable and lasts for the long-term.

Why Envision?

We at Enactus VIT believe that we are capable of generating employment for the visually challenged. Our vision is to make the visually impaired self sufficient by providing them the skills and means to earn through a BPO.

How does our project aim to attain sustainability?

Project Envision will involve the hiring of visually challenged people in BPOs. Once our project moves past its pilot stage, the employees would be able to make calls from their own houses. We would help them establish direct contact with their employer and then, de-link ourselves from the project.

What are the goals and objectives of Project Envision?

  • PROVIDE Fulfilling the telemarketing requirement of a company and providing the customers with telemarketing services.

  • EMPOWER a community of visually challenged so as to augment and stabilise their income.

Can I ( a visually impaired person ) living outside Vellore be a part of this project?

Currently, we are in the testing phase. Once we branch out, people from outside would be allowed to join our team.
